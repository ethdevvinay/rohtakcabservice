Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before booking your ride.

1. Booking Confirmation

Your booking is confirmed only when you receive a confirmation message via WhatsApp or SMS from Rohtak Cab Service. We reserve the right to cancel any booking if a vehicle is not available due to unforeseen circumstances.

2. Pricing & Tolls

The total fare includes driver allowance and fuel charges. Toll taxes, parking fees, and state entry taxes (if any) are to be paid extra by the customer directly during the trip. Night charges may apply for trips between 10:00 PM and 5:00 AM.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation if cancelled 4 hours prior to the pickup time.
  • 50% cancellation charge if cancelled within 4 hours of pickup time.
  • No refund if the customer does not show up at the pickup location without prior notice.

4. Passenger Safety & Conduct

We prioritize passenger safety. Consumption of alcohol or illegal substances inside the cab is strictly prohibited. The driver reserves the right to refuse service if passengers are found intoxicated or misbehaving.

5. Liability

Rohtak Cab Service is not liable for any delays caused by traffic jams, road blocks, weather conditions, or mechanic failure. We will try our best to arrange an alternate vehicle in case of breakdown.

6. Disputes

Any disputes arising out of the taxi services shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts in Rohtak, Haryana.
